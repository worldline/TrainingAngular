Main Concepts Practical Work 2 - Services

Let's play with some services, essentially http, to implement our own NewsService service.

  • Have a look at NewsService:
    • Test your dev directly on the main page
    • Complete the getNews method by using the http service: method is GET & url is '/api/app/news'. Be sure to keep an instance of news instead of call http everytime
    • Complete the addLike method: method POST & url is '/api/app/news/like/(news id)'. The post doesn't need specific data. Remember to update the number of likes on the local news when http answers successfully
    • Complete addNews: method POST & url is /api/app/news & POST content is the news itself. Update your local news on success.
    • Complete deleteNews: method DELETE & url is /api/app/news/' followed by the news id. Update your local news.
    • Complete randomNews: method GET & url is '/api/app/news/random'
