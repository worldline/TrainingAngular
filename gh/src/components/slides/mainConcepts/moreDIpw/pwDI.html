Main Concepts Practical Work 5- Services & Dependency Injection

You will create a simple service and inject it into a component

  • To do so, create and declare the service ContactService. The service has to contain informations which are actually hardly coded in the component: email, phone, address
  • Inject the service into your component and use its field to initialize your component fields
  • If you declared your service into the providers of a component, check that it also work if you declare it on the bootstrap method, and vice versa

The service we are creating is very simple, in a real world, it would at less retrieve these informations from the server and call an API to post the message, and so on. The idea here is just to let declare your own service and inject it into your component.
