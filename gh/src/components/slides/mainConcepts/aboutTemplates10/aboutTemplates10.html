Main Concepts About Templates - Declare your components

  • You can use core Components/Directives into your templates without extra work
  • But you need to declare them if you want to use custom Components
  • To do that, there is a field named declarations that you have to use on your @NgModule annotation
  • import {CustomComponent} from './custom-cmp'; @NgModule({ bootstrap:[...], imports:[...], declarations:[CustomComponent, ...] })
