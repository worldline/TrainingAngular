Ecosystem Start From Scratch

There are many options to start a new Angular app. The aim here is to show you the alternatives, how and when to use them.

Here are the points let's discuss about:

  • Which language will you choose to start your project ? Will you prefer typescript, ES6 or ES5
  • No matter what language you choose, it will be transpiled in ES5 to be executed on your browser, what are the ways to achieve this?
  • Depending on the choices you do, some polyfills will be needed, let's have a look at these polyfills
